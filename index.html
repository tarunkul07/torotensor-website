Agent stack

Specialized agents that work together.

One universal agent is not enough for real business operations. The platform deploys task-specific agents around the client’s workflow.

Flow MapperTurns the client’s current process into a workflow map with roles, rules, and outcomes.
Customer Operations AgentHandles enquiries, lead capture, follow-ups, task routing, and owner summaries.
Quotation AgentDrafts quotations, checks required details, and sends for approval before delivery.
Document AgentReads business PDFs, forms, SOPs, invoices, brochures, and policy documents.
Task AgentAssigns work, tracks responsibility, highlights delays, and keeps teams accountable.
Support AgentClassifies support requests, drafts replies, escalates urgent cases, and tracks resolution.
Payment Reminder AgentTracks pending amounts and prepares polite reminders with human approval.
Owner Report AgentGenerates daily summaries, risk alerts, pipeline view, and pending-action reports.

FAQ

Common questions before a guided demo.

BusinessAgent OS is positioned as a productized workflow layer, not a one-off custom software project.

Can it work for any business?

It can work wherever the business has repeatable workflows, incoming requests, documents, tasks, approvals, follow-ups, or owner reporting needs.

Will it replace staff?

No. The product is designed to support staff with capture, drafting, reminders, tracking, and reporting while keeping humans in control.

How is this different from general AI agents?

The focus is business-flow mapping, approval rules, business memory, dashboards, and measurable operational outcomes.

Can we start without full integrations?

Yes. A pilot can start with sample data, forms, spreadsheets, email exports, and documents. Deeper integrations can be added later.
